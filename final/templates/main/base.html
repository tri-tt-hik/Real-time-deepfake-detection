Welcome to the Deepfake Detection Portal

The rise of deepfake technology has brought incredible advancements in media creation but also significant challenges in distinguishing real from manipulated content. Our portal provides tools and resources to help individuals, organizations, and researchers detect and analyze deepfake content.

Why Deepfake Detection Matters

  • Combating Misinformation: Deepfakes can be used to spread false information, creating confusion and eroding trust.
  • Protecting Privacy: Deepfakes can manipulate videos to harm individuals or tarnish reputations.
  • Ensuring Authenticity: Verifying the authenticity of media is crucial in today's digital age.
